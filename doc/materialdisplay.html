typeCase widgets: MaterialDisplay

The MaterialDisplay shows the properties of a single material. It shows the name of the material, the speed of light in this material (in units of the speed of light in vacuum), the materials density and it's decomposition into elements. Additionally parameters for a energyLoss-algorithm can be provided.

This display can be used in normal or read-only-mode, where in read-only-mode, the user cannot modify the properties of the material..
