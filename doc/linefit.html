    • float lineFit::fit(int numPoints, vector3D* points, vector3D* resolution, float* sigmas, sLine3D& result)
      Static method. Fits a line to numPoints points in 3D. The resolution-vectors are the vectors in which the points can be modified, the sigmas, the errors for the points.
      Take care, that the arrays points, resolution and sigmas are at least numPoints indeces long.
      The result of the fit is given by the straight line result, where the Foot of the line is always in the x-y-plane.
      The return value is the chi² value of the fit.
