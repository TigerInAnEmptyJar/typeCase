typeCase widgets: DetectorWidget

The DetectorWidget defines the geometrical detector-setup. Again on the left there is a list of all defined sub-detectors of your current experimental setup.

On the right, the upper part shows the current sub-detector in a DetectorDisplay, including a visualisation of its shape (total detector in green, first element in red).

The items in the list on the left are checkable. All checked sub-detectors are displayed in a root-TCanvas.
